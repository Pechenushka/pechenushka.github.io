01 Overview

IceFort Wallet (the "App") is a mobile cryptocurrency wallet application published by SIRELIS AI LTD ("we", "us", or "our") under the package identifier com.swgaltech.icefortwalletapp. The App is available for Android and iOS devices.

The App enables you to manage cryptocurrency assets on Ethereum, Tron, Polygon, and Binance Smart Chain networks using NFC-enabled hardware security cards for secure key storage and transaction signing.

This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes, where it is stored, and which third-party services are involved.

02 Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone number, address, government ID)
  • Private keys, seed phrases, or signing secrets
  • User account credentials (there is no account or registration system)
  • Location or GPS data
  • Contacts, call logs, or messages
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history or cookies
  • Biometric templates (fingerprint, face geometry)
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings

03 Data Stored on Your Device

All wallet data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's application preferences. No wallet data is sent to our servers. We do not operate any backend servers or databases.

04 Network Communications

The App communicates with public blockchain infrastructure to provide wallet functionality. The following types of network requests are made:

Blockchain RPC Nodes

The App sends read-only queries and transaction broadcasts to public blockchain RPC nodes. These requests include your public blockchain address (which is pseudonymous, not personally identifiable). Default RPC providers include:

Network Provider
Ethereum PublicNode, LlamaRPC
Tron TronGrid (by Tron Foundation)
Polygon polygon-rpc.com
BNB Smart Chain Binance public RPC

You may configure custom RPC endpoints in the App settings if you prefer to route requests through your own node.

Transaction History

Transaction history is fetched from blockchain indexing services (Etherscan API, TronGrid API). Your public address is included in these API queries to retrieve your transaction records.

Exchange Rate Data

The App fetches current cryptocurrency-to-USD exchange rates from the WhiteBIT public ticker API. No user data or wallet information is included in these requests.

Blockchain Transparency Notice

All blockchain transactions are recorded on public, immutable ledgers. Transaction amounts, sender and recipient addresses, and timestamps are publicly visible on the respective blockchain networks. This is inherent to how public blockchains operate and is not specific to this App.

06 Crash Reporting & Diagnostics

The App uses Sentry for crash reporting and error diagnostics. Sentry data is processed in the European Union (Sentry's EU data center).

What Sentry may receive:

  • Application crash reports and stack traces
  • Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • NFC communication diagnostic data (card response codes, connection status)
  • Error messages generated during app operation

What Sentry does NOT receive:

  • Private keys or signing secrets
  • PIN codes or PIN hashes
  • Wallet balances or transaction amounts
  • Personal identity information

Sentry data is used exclusively for identifying and fixing software defects. For more information, see Sentry's Privacy Policy.

08 NFC Card & Cryptographic Data

The App is designed to work with NFC-enabled hardware security cards for cryptographic key storage.

Security Architecture

Private keys are generated and stored exclusively on the NFC hardware card. The App never has access to, extracts, or transmits private keys. All transaction signing occurs on the card itself. The App only sends a transaction hash to the card and receives a cryptographic signature in return.

Data exchanged with the NFC card:

  • Public keys — read from the card to derive wallet addresses
  • Transaction hashes — sent to the card for on-card ECDSA signing
  • Signatures — received from the card after signing
  • PIN verification — the PIN you enter is transmitted directly to the card's secure element for on-card verification
  • Card UID — a hardware identifier used to associate wallets with specific cards

All NFC communication happens locally between your phone and the card held against it. No NFC data is relayed over the internet.

09 Biometric Authentication

The App offers optional biometric unlock (fingerprint or Face ID) as a convenience feature.

  • Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android BiometricPrompt or iOS LocalAuthentication).
  • The App receives only a pass/fail result from the OS.
  • No biometric templates, fingerprint data, or facial geometry are accessed, stored, or transmitted by the App.

10 Camera & QR Code Scanning

The camera is used exclusively for scanning QR codes that contain wallet addresses.

  • Camera frames are processed locally on your device in real time.
  • No images or video are stored to disk, uploaded, or transmitted.
  • The camera is activated only when you explicitly open the QR scanner.
  • Decoded QR data is validated as a blockchain address format before use.

11 Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Hardware key isolation — private keys never leave the NFC security card's secure element
  • PIN protection — the App PIN is stored as a salted SHA-256 hash, not in plaintext
  • Automatic session timeout — the App locks after 5 minutes of inactivity
  • PIN lockout — access is temporarily blocked after multiple failed PIN attempts
  • No remote data storage — wallet data exists only on your device and NFC card
  • HTTPS encryption — all network communication uses encrypted connections
Your Responsibility

Because this is a self-custody wallet, you are solely responsible for the security of your NFC card, PIN, and any backup seed phrases. If you lose your NFC card and do not have a backup, your funds cannot be recovered by us or anyone else.
